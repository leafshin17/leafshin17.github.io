About us

Sustainify is a new website dedicated to helping connect students and faculty across the University of Waterloo campus to sustainable projects and initiatives. Sustainify hopes that clubs, groups, stores, restaurants and event organizers will share and create on-campus events and destinations for both students and faculty to create a more sustainable UW.

How it works

Event organizers and Club Leaders

Clubs and event organizers will sign up for an account on Sustainify to create and publish sustainability-focused events on our website. These events will be put on the campus map and all users will be able to find out more information about this event and register to take part. After the event takes place, users will be able to check-in with the hosts and to cofirm event attendance. Here's the catch: we have rewards for users who distinguish themselves and participate actively.
