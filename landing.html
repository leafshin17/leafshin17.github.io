About Us

Sustainify is a new website dedicated to helping connect students and faculty across the University of Waterloo campus to sustainable projects and initiatives. Sustainify hopes that clubs, groups, stores, restaurants and event organizers will share and create on-campus events and destinations for both students and faculty to create a more sustainable UW. Here's the perk: we have rewards for users who distinguish themselves and participate actively.

How It Works

New Users

Whether you want to attend sustainability events, find local businesses that support this initiative, or would like to find the nearest green transportation, Sustainify is for you! Register with us to be an active user and receive rewards for your participation.

Event organizers and Club Leaders

Clubs and event organizers will sign up for an account on Sustainify to create and publish sustainability-focused events on our website. These events will be put on the campus map and all users will be able to find out more information about this event and register to take part. After the event takes place, users will be able to check-in with the hosts and to cofirm event attendance.

Sustainable Businesses

Sustainify encourages business owners of all kinds to register to become a sustainable business and receive marked locations on our website's map. We also ecourage owners to sponser incentives and rewards for active users, to promote business traffic and success in our sustainability efforts.
